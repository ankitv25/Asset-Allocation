The construction principle

Nothing is held because a mean-variance optimizer pointed at it. Each instrument enters through a role (core return engine, diversifier, safe-haven anchor, or tilt), is grouped into a role-anchored sleeve, and is sized inside governed floors and ceilings. The optimizer informs the weights; the investment thesis justifies the position.
